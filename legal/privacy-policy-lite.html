2. About Offline Player Lite

Offline Player Lite is a streamlined, lightweight music player designed for users who prefer a simpler experience with reduced storage footprint. While it offers fewer features than the full Offline Player app, we maintain the same commitment to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve the Lite experience.

3. Information We Collect

Offline Player Lite collects the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Basic information on how the app is used, including feature access and interaction patterns. Lite collects less usage data than the full version due to its simplified feature set.
  • Music Data: Information about songs played and playlists. This data is stored locally on your device. We may collect aggregated, non-identifiable statistics for service improvement.
  • Device Information: Operating system version and unique device identifiers for compatibility and crash diagnostics.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain Offline Player Lite.
  • Notify you about updates and changes.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Monitor usage and improve app performance.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only:

  • For Legal Requirements: When required by law or valid requests by public authorities.
