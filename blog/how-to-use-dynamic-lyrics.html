How To Use Dynamic Lyrics

Learn how to enable and enjoy perfectly synchronized lyrics that scroll in real-time with your music. Turn every song into a karaoke experience.

What are Dynamic Lyrics?

Dynamic Lyrics are time-synchronized lyrics that highlight word-by-word as the song plays. Unlike static lyrics, they automatically scroll and highlight the current line, making it easy to follow along and sing along with your favorite songs.

1

Start Playing a Song

Open Offline Player and start playing any song from your library. Dynamic Lyrics work automatically for songs that have synchronized lyric data available.

Note: Most popular songs have dynamic lyrics available. For less common tracks, static lyrics may be shown instead.

2

Open Lyrics View

While a song is playing, tap the Lyrics icon (microphone or text icon) on the Now Playing screen. This will open the Dynamic Lyrics view.

  • Lyrics icon is located in the bottom toolbar of the Now Playing screen
  • You can also swipe up on the Now Playing screen to reveal lyrics
  • Lyrics appear automatically if enabled in settings
3

Navigate Lyrics

Once the lyrics are displayed, you'll see them scroll automatically. The current line is highlighted in your theme's accent color, and the app automatically scrolls to keep the active line visible.

Auto-Scroll

Lyrics scroll automatically to follow the song

Manual Scroll

Swipe up/down to manually browse lyrics

Word Highlighting

Current word is highlighted in real-time

Line Highlighting

Active line uses accent color

4

Use Full-Screen Mode

For the best karaoke experience, tap the Full-Screen button (usually in the top-right corner of the lyrics view). This expands the lyrics to fill your entire screen with a beautiful, distraction-free display.

  • Full-screen mode hides all UI elements except lyrics
  • Perfect for karaoke sessions or focused listening
  • Tap anywhere or swipe down to exit full-screen
5

Customize Lyrics Settings

Go to Settings â†’ Lyrics to customize your lyrics experience:

  • Enable/disable automatic lyrics display
  • Adjust font size for better readability
  • Choose lyrics alignment (left, center, right)
  • Toggle word-by-word highlighting
  • Set auto-scroll speed

Pro Tips

  • Karaoke Mode: Use full-screen lyrics with external speakers for the ultimate karaoke experience.
  • Language Learning: Follow along with lyrics to improve pronunciation and vocabulary in foreign languages.
  • Lyrics Search: Use the search function to find specific lines or words in the lyrics.
  • Offline Access: Lyrics are cached with songs, so they work offline too!
