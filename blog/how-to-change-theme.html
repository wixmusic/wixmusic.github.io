How To Change Theme

Personalize Offline Player with custom themes, colors, and background images. Make the app truly yours with our Liquid Glass design system.

Personalize Your Music Experience

Offline Player's Liquid Glass design allows you to fully customize the app's appearance. Change theme colors, accent hues, and background images to match your style and mood. The translucent Liquid Glass effect adapts beautifully to any customization you choose. Combine with Smart Playlists and audio settings for a fully personalized experience.

1

Open Appearance Settings

Launch Offline Player and go to Settings â†’ Appearance. This is where all theme customization options are located.

Quick Tip: You can also access Appearance settings from the main menu by tapping your profile icon.

2

Choose Theme Color

Tap on "Theme Color" to open the color picker. You can either:

  • Select from preset color schemes (Blue, Purple, Green, Red, etc.)
  • Use the color wheel to choose any custom color
  • Enter a hex color code for precise color matching

The theme color affects UI accents, buttons, and highlight colors throughout the app.

3

Set Accent Color

The accent color is used for special highlights and interactive elements. Choose a complementary color that works well with your theme color. You can preview the combination in real-time.

Complementary Colors

Choose colors opposite on the color wheel for contrast

Analogous Colors

Choose adjacent colors for harmony

4

Customize Background Image

Tap "Background Image" to set a custom backdrop. You can:

  • Choose from built-in background presets
  • Select a photo from your device's photo library
  • Use a solid color background
  • Enable blur effect for the Liquid Glass aesthetic

The Liquid Glass effect creates a beautiful translucent overlay over your background image.

5

Apply and Preview

After customizing your theme, tap "Apply" to save your changes. The app will immediately update with your new theme. You can always revert to the default theme or create multiple theme presets.

  • Save multiple theme presets for different moods
  • Quick switch between themes from settings
  • Share your custom themes with friends

Pro Tips

  • Contrast Matters: Ensure text remains readable with your chosen background and colors.
  • Liquid Glass Effect: The translucent effect works best with vibrant, colorful backgrounds.
  • Battery Consideration: Complex backgrounds may slightly impact battery life.
  • Seasonal Themes: Create different themes for different times of day or seasons.
