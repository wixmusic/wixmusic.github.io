What is Max Volume Boost?

Max Volume Boost uses advanced digital signal processing to safely amplify audio beyond your device's standard volume limit. Combined with our professional 10-band Equalizer, you can achieve the perfect sound for any genre or listening environment. Learn more about the Volume Boost feature or explore the latest Top Hits Charts to test your audio settings.

Important: Use volume boost responsibly. Prolonged exposure to high volumes can damage your hearing.

1

Access Audio Settings

Open Offline Player and navigate to Settings → Audio. Here you'll find both the Volume Boost and Equalizer controls.

Quick Access: You can also access audio settings from the Now Playing screen by tapping the EQ icon.

2

Enable Max Volume Boost

Toggle on the "Max Volume Boost" switch. You'll immediately notice an increase in volume. Adjust the boost level using the slider (recommended: start at 50% and adjust to your preference).

  • Start with a lower boost level and gradually increase
  • Test with different headphones or speakers
  • Monitor for any distortion or clipping
3

Customize the Equalizer

Below the Volume Boost, you'll find the 10-band Equalizer. Adjust each frequency band to match your preferences. Perfect your sound for different genres - try the December Top Hits Charts to test various music styles:

Low Frequencies (32Hz - 125Hz)

Bass control - boost for more punch, reduce for clarity

Mid Frequencies (500Hz - 2kHz)

Vocals and instruments - adjust for presence

High Frequencies (4kHz - 16kHz)

Treble and clarity - boost for brightness

Presets Available

Rock, Pop, Classical, Jazz, and more

4

Save Your Settings

Once you've found your perfect audio settings, tap "Save" to apply them. Your settings will be remembered for all future playback.

  • Create custom EQ presets for different music genres
  • Save different profiles for headphones vs speakers
  • Quick switch between presets from the Now Playing screen

Pro Tips

  • Start Low: Begin with moderate boost levels and gradually increase to find your comfort zone.
  • Genre-Specific EQ: Create different EQ presets for different music genres (bass-heavy for EDM, balanced for pop). Combine with Smart Playlists to automatically organize music by genre. Test your presets with trending songs from our Top Hits Charts.
  • Headphone Matching: Adjust EQ based on your headphone's frequency response characteristics. For the best experience, customize your appearance settings to match your listening preferences.
  • Safety First: Use the built-in volume limiter to protect your hearing and speakers. Learn more about audio settings in our complete tutorials guide.
