How To Download from Dropbox

Learn how to import and play music files directly from your Dropbox account in Offline Player.

Import Music from Dropbox

Offline Player seamlessly integrates with Dropbox, allowing you to access and import your music files stored in the cloud. This feature makes it convenient to sync your music collection across devices. Once imported, organize your music with Smart Playlists, enhance the listening experience with Volume Boost and Dynamic Lyrics, or discover new music from our Top Hits Charts and Portuguese Top Hits.

Supported Formats: MP3, M4A, FLAC, WAV, and other common audio formats.

1

Access Import Options

Open Offline Player and tap the "+" button or go to Settings â†’ Import Music. You'll see a list of cloud storage options including Dropbox.

Tip: The import menu is also accessible from the main library screen via the menu icon.

2

Connect Dropbox Account

Select "Dropbox" from the import options. You'll be redirected to sign in to your Dropbox account. Tap "Sign in with Dropbox" and authorize Offline Player to access your files.

  • Ensure you're logged into the correct Dropbox account
  • Grant file access permissions when prompted
  • Your authentication is securely handled and encrypted
3

Navigate and Select Music

After connecting, browse your Dropbox folders to locate music files. The interface allows you to:

Explore Folders

Browse through your Dropbox directory structure to find music files

Quick Search

Use the search bar to find specific songs, albums, or artists quickly

Multi-Select

Select multiple files or entire folders for batch import

4

Start Import Process

Once you've selected your music files, tap "Import" or "Add to Library". The app will begin downloading the selected files from Dropbox to your device.

  • Track import progress in real-time via notifications
  • Large files or multiple files may require more time
  • The app remains fully functional during the import process
5

Enjoy Your Music

When the import completes, your music will be available in your library. You can access it through:

All Songs - Complete list of imported tracks

Albums - Music organized by album

Artists - Songs grouped by artist

Pro Tips

  • Folder Structure: Maintain organized folders in Dropbox for easier music discovery and import.
  • Efficient Importing: Import entire music folders at once to streamline the process for large collections.
  • Storage Awareness: Keep track of your device's available storage space when importing large files.
  • Sync Updates: Re-import from Dropbox whenever you add new music files to keep your library up to date.
