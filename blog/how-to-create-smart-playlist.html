What are Smart Playlists?

Smart Playlists automatically organize your music library based on criteria like artist, genre, mood, or listening frequency. They update automatically as you add new songs, keeping your library perfectly organized without any manual work. Once you've imported music from Google Drive or Dropbox, Smart Playlists will automatically organize them. Learn more about the Smart Playlist feature or discover trending music from our Top Hits Charts to add to your playlists.

1

Access Playlist Management

Open Offline Player and navigate to the Playlists section. Tap the "+" or "Create Playlist" button to start creating a new playlist.

Tip: Smart Playlists are automatically created for each artist in your library. You can also create custom smart playlists.

2

Choose Smart Playlist Type

Select "Smart Playlist" from the creation options. You'll see several automatic organization types:

By Artist

Automatically groups all songs by each artist

By Genre

Organizes music by genre (Pop, Rock, Jazz, etc.)

By Mood

Creates playlists based on song mood (Happy, Chill, Energetic)

Most Played

Groups your most frequently played songs

3

Configure Smart Playlist Rules

After selecting a type, you can customize the rules for your Smart Playlist. For example:

  • Artist Playlists: Automatically include all songs by selected artists
  • Genre Playlists: Choose specific genres to include or exclude. Perfect for using with genre-specific EQ presets. Explore popular genres from our December Top Hits Charts or Portuguese Top Hits.
  • Mood Playlists: Select mood tags (Workout, Relax, Party, etc.)
  • Date Range: Include songs added within a specific time period. Works great with music imported from Google Drive or Dropbox. Perfect for organizing tracks from our latest Top Hits Charts.
  • Play Count: Filter by minimum or maximum play counts

You can combine multiple rules to create highly specific Smart Playlists.

4

Name and Save Your Playlist

Give your Smart Playlist a descriptive name (e.g., "My Rock Collection" or "Chill Vibes Only"). Tap "Create" to save it. The playlist will immediately populate with songs matching your criteria.

  • Smart Playlists update automatically when you add new songs
  • You can manually add or remove songs if needed
  • Edit playlist rules anytime from playlist settings
5

Manage and Edit Smart Playlists

To edit a Smart Playlist, tap the three-dot menu (⋯) next to the playlist name and select "Edit Rules". You can:

  • Modify existing rules or add new ones
  • Change the playlist name and cover art
  • Set playlist to auto-update or manual mode
  • Delete the Smart Playlist if no longer needed

Pro Tips

  • Auto-Artist Playlists: Offline Player automatically creates playlists for every artist in your library - no setup needed!
  • Combine Rules: Use multiple criteria (e.g., "Rock songs from 2020-2024") for precise organization.
  • Regular Updates: Smart Playlists refresh automatically, so new songs matching your criteria appear instantly.
  • Export Playlists: Share your Smart Playlists with friends or export them for backup.
