How To Offline

Learn how to enable and use automatic offline caching in Offline Player. Never lose your music again, even without internet connection.

What is Automatic Offline Caching?

Offline Player automatically saves every song you play to your device's local storage. This means you can listen to your music even when you don't have an internet connection. Unlike other apps like Snaptube that require manual downloads, Offline Player does this automatically in the background.

1

Open Offline Player

Launch the Offline Player app on your iOS device. The automatic caching feature is enabled by default, so you don't need to configure anything.

Tip: Automatic caching works in the background as you listen to music.

2

Play Your Music

Simply start playing any song from your library. As the song plays, Offline Player automatically saves it to your device's cache. You'll see a small indicator when a song is being cached.

  • Play songs normally - no need to tap "Download"
  • Caching happens automatically in the background
  • First play streams online, subsequent plays use cached version
3

Verify Offline Access

To test offline playback, turn on Airplane Mode or disconnect from Wi-Fi. Then try playing a song you've listened to before. It should play instantly from the cache without any buffering.

Note: Only songs you've played at least once will be available offline.

4

Manage Cache Storage

You can manage your cached songs in Settings. Navigate to Settings → Storage → Cached Music to view and manage your offline library.

  • View total cached storage usage
  • Clear cache for specific songs or all songs
  • Set automatic cache limits
  • Pin favorite songs to always keep cached

Pro Tips

  • Wi-Fi First: Play songs on Wi-Fi to avoid using mobile data for caching.
  • Storage Management: Regularly check your cache size to ensure you have enough storage space.
  • Favorite Songs: Pin your favorite songs in settings to ensure they're always available offline.
  • Automatic Cleanup: Offline Player automatically manages cache based on your listening frequency.
