Product Updates

A chronological history of new features, performance enhancements, and fixes for the Go Play Music app.

Version 1.1.0: Smart Playlist Engine & Hi-Res Audio Support

Released: July 15, 2025

  • NEW: Introduced the Smart Playlist Creator, automatically grouping music by artist and mood.
  • NEW: Added native support for FLAC and $\text{ALAC}$ high-resolution audio formats.
  • IMPROVEMENT: Enhanced the speed and reliability of the Automatic Offline Caching feature.
  • FIX: Resolved an issue where the equalizer settings occasionally reset upon app restart.

Version 1.0.5: Stability and UI Polish

Released: June 20, 2025

  • IMPROVEMENT: Reduced application startup time by $20\%$.
  • FIX: Addressed a crash related to rapid playlist switching.
  • FIX: Minor visual adjustments to the Frosted Glass effect opacity on older $\text{iOS}$ devices.

Version 1.0.0: Initial Launch

Released: June 1, 2025

  • NEW: Automatic Offline Caching, Volume Boost, Frosted Glass UI, and Dynamic Lyrics.
