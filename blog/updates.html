Product Updates

A chronological history of new features, performance enhancements, and fixes for the Offline Player app. See why users are switching from Snaptube to Offline Player for better offline music experience.

Version 1.1.0: Smart Playlist Engine & Hi-Res Audio Support

Released: July 15, 2026

  • NEW: Introduced the Smart Playlist Creator, automatically grouping music by artist and mood. Learn how to use it in our Smart Playlist tutorial. Organize songs from our Top Hits Charts with Smart Playlists.
  • NEW: Added native support for FLAC and $\text{ALAC}$ high-resolution audio formats.
  • FIX: Resolved an issue where the equalizer settings occasionally reset upon app restart. See our Volume Boost guide for EQ setup tips.

Version 1.0.5: Stability and UI Polish

Released: June 20, 2026

  • IMPROVEMENT: Reduced application startup time by $20\%$.
  • FIX: Addressed a crash related to rapid playlist switching.
  • FIX: Minor visual adjustments to the Liquid Glass effect opacity on older $\text{iOS}$ devices.

Version 1.0.0: Initial Launch

Released: June 1, 2026

  • NEW: Volume Boost, Liquid Glass UI, and Dynamic Lyrics. Check out our tutorials to master these features, or test them with trending songs from our Top Hits Charts.
