How Does It Work?

  • **Real-Time Caching:** As the song streams, the data is concurrently written to local storage.
  • **Intelligent Management:** Cache space is automatically optimized based on your playback frequency and device storage to ensure important songs are always available.
  • **Seamless Transition:** When the network signal is lost, the app switches to local cached playback in milliseconds, ensuring zero interruption.
