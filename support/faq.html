Offline & Caching

Offline Player automatically saves a copy of every song you listen to onto your device. This means the second time you play it, it loads instantly from local storage, eliminating buffering and ensuring uninterrupted playback, regardless of network stability.

Yes. While caching is automatic by default, you can manage your stored songs in the app settings, allowing you to clear older tracks or manually pin favorites to ensure they are always available.

Sound & Volume

Max Volume Boost is designed to optimize the digital signal processing without causing immediate hardware damage. However, we strongly recommend using this feature responsibly and avoiding extremely high volumes for extended periods to protect your hearing and speaker health.

Our Equalizer offers 10 bands of adjustment, along with several professional pre-sets (Rock, Pop, Classical, etc.). You can fine-tune frequencies from bass to treble to perfectly match your headphones or listening environment.
